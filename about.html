About Me

Hello! 👋

I’m Fiona. I’m a UX designer and illustrator from Edinburgh. I have a background in creative arts and have always been curious about design psychology.

In my free time I love getting outdoors and exploring new places, getting to gigs and stand up comedy, and wandering like a tourist in my home city!

Journey into UX 🗺

I’ve travelled into UX via a degree specialising in jewellery design followed by over ten years in retail display. Alongside, I’ve worked on personal projects like running a creative business selling my artwork. This mix of experience has given me in-depth awareness of designing for people’s varying needs, giving attention to both emotional and practical considerations. Whether it’s for the wearer, customer, or viewer, designing for people has always been at the heart of what I do.

Wanting to build on this interest, I enrolled on an immersive one year's UX Design Certifcation with CareerFoundry which gave me hands-on experience right across the user-centred design process. Following a specialisation in Frontend Development for Designers, I have been building up my knowledge of user research as part of a team non-profit venture.

Moving into UX, I would love to bring my skills to collaborate on innovative projects that can have a positive impact on people’s lives and the world around us.
